About LEV

LEV – Eco Village is an intentional community in Costa Rica rooted in regenerative living, shared values, and a deep respect for the natural world. Set within a lush tropical landscape, LEV is designed as a place where people can live lightly on the land while building meaningful connections with each other.


At LEV, we believe that sustainable living is not only about ecological responsibility, but also about community, creativity, and personal growth. Our village is shaped around principles of permaculture, thoughtful design, and cooperation — creating a balanced environment that supports both individual autonomy and collective wellbeing.


Life at LEV invites a slower, more conscious rhythm. Residents and visitors alike are encouraged to engage with the land, participate in shared activities, and contribute their skills and ideas to the evolving fabric of the community. From shared meals and collaborative projects to quiet moments in nature, LEV offers space for both connection and reflection.


Whether you are seeking a long-term home, a place to learn and grow, or a temporary retreat immersed in nature, LEV – Eco Village offers a living example of what is possible when people come together with intention, care, and respect for the Earth.

Our Vision

At La Ecovilla, our vision is to cultivate a regenerative, human-centered way of living where people, land, and community evolve together. We seek to create a living example of what is possible when ecological responsibility, cooperation, and shared purpose form the foundation of daily life.

Regenerative Design

We go beyond sustainability by designing homes, landscapes, and systems that actively restore ecosystems and enhance biodiversity.

Conscious Community

La Ecovilla is rooted in cooperation, transparency, and mutual respect — fostering a culture of connection, learning, and shared responsibility.

Local Resilience

Through local food production, renewable energy, and resilient systems, we aim to support a self-reliant lifestyle aligned with the rhythms of nature.

Life at La Ecovilla

Life at La Ecovilla unfolds at a slower, more intentional pace — shaped by the rhythms of nature, shared purpose, and daily moments of connection. Set within Costa Rica’s rich and vibrant landscape, the village offers a way of living that balances individual freedom with collective care.


Community life is woven through simple, meaningful experiences: shared meals made from locally grown food, collaborative projects on the land, creative workshops, and spontaneous conversations that turn neighbors into friends. Participation is encouraged but never forced, allowing each person to engage in ways that feel natural and aligned.


Whether tending gardens, learning new skills, or enjoying quiet time surrounded by nature, life at La Ecovilla invites presence, contribution, and reflection. It is a place where people come not just to live, but to grow — together, and in harmony with the land that supports them.

Useful Information

Living in Costa Rica — and within an eco village — is a deeply rewarding experience, but it comes with its own rhythms, systems, and expectations. This section offers practical insights to help residents and visitors feel grounded, prepared, and at home.

Life in Costa Rica

Costa Rica operates at a slower, relationship-centered pace often referred to as “Pura Vida.” Patience, flexibility, and respect go a long way — whether navigating local services, transportation, or daily interactions.

Climate & Nature

Expect warm temperatures year-round, a lush green rainy season, and abundant wildlife. Living close to nature means embracing rain, insects, and changing conditions as part of the experience.

Eco-Village Living

Life at LEV values shared responsibility, open communication, and mindful resource use. Residents are encouraged to participate in community activities while respecting personal boundaries and diverse lifestyles.

Resources & Self-Reliance

Eco village life often involves alternative systems such as rainwater collection, composting, and renewable energy. Awareness and care help ensure these systems remain resilient and sustainable.

Health & Wellbeing

Costa Rica offers excellent healthcare, both public and private. At LEV, wellbeing is also supported through connection to nature, community support, and conscious daily practices.

Respecting the Land

Stewardship of the land is central to life at LEV. This includes mindful water use, waste reduction, respect for wildlife, and participation in regenerative land practices.
